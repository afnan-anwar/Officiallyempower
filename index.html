About Us

Empower is a youth-led global nonprofit organization creating inclusive debate spaces where every voice is welcomed regardless of background, culture, or context.


We believe debate and public speaking are more than competitions; they build confidence, critical thinking, leadership, and communication skills that prepare young people to make a differenece in every field of life.
