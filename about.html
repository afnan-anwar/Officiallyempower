About Us

Empower is a nonprofit youth-led global organization pushing against exclusivity in debate spaces making room for all voices to speak regardless of thier background culture or context.

We are a community of young people who believe in the power of debate and public speaking to change the world. We provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for young people to develop their skills and make a difference in their communities.

Debating and public speaking sharpens our ability to think on our feet and respond logically. They aren't just for competitions - they build confidence, negotation skills, and critical thinking that carry over into every aspect of our lives. Whether someone ends up in politics, business, or art, the ability to speak clearly, lead thoughtfully, and challenge ideas is something that will always serve them.

Empower is more than just a debate organization, we are building a generation of independednt thinkers and future changemakers by providing a space where people connect with others around the world, across borders, backgrounds and beliefs. Learning to collaborate with people from different cultures and contexts sharpens perspective and opens doors. Knowing how to speak across borders is an asset, a skill more valuable than ever in today's world.

Empower is not just about skill, but about community, growth, and preparing for a world where communication is everything. It's not just about speaking - it's about learning to speak. Mistakes are welcome. Whether you are a beginner or seasoned speaker, this is the place for you.
