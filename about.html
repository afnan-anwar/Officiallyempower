Our Mission

Empower is a youth-led global nonprofit organization creating inclusive debate spaces where every voice is welcomed, regardless of background, culture, or context.

Why Debate Matters

We believe debate and public speaking are more than competitions. They build confidence, critical thinking, leadership, and communication skills that prepare young people to make a difference in every field of life.

Our Community

Empower is a global community where students connect across cultures, learn from one another, and grow into independent thinkers and changemakers. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced speaker, this is a place to lean, grow, and belong.
